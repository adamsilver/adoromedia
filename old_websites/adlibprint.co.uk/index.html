Welcome to the website of Ad Lib Print Shop.

Ad Lib is a print and design company, established in the industry since 1983.

If you call, you will be greeted by, and deal directly with, one of the directors. We know that the personal service we provide is as important to us as it is to you.

Our large client-base consists of personal, private and corporate business customers alike. The 100 black on white letterheads for home are just as important as the 10,000 full colour brochures for corporate companies (although the latter does take a little longer!!!).

Apart from printing we also provide general photocopying services in black and white or colour, in quantities from 1 to 1000s.

This site provides information on the services Ad Lib can provide for you, the customer. You can find out where we are situated and request quotes by calling or using our online enquiry form.

Do you need a brochure or e-commerce website? If so, call us to find out more details, and be surprised at the very competitive prices.

We will help you with the design of your printed items, or you can supply us with your design on disk or send it by e-mail. We use high-speed ADSL for easy file transfer.
