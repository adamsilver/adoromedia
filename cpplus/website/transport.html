Car park management - transport

CP Plus is a market leader in the transport sector, managing parking at national motorway service stations and mainline railway station parking. Since contracting out the parking management and operation of their car & lorry parks to CP Plus, some of the biggest names in UK transport have seen their parking revenues increase substantially and their parking assets controlled and managed more professionally.

Parking Management on the Road - we are No.1 managing parking at motorway service stations throughout the UK on behalf of all the major service area providers. We offer similar car park management services for Park and Ride schemes, both private and local authority run.

Rail

Parking Management for Rail - CP Plus manages more than a hundred network sites for South West Trains. Our management systems allow TOCs to provide an enhanced service to their travelling customers and cut down on parking abuse, while ensuring they receive maximum return on investment from their parking assets. The charging, security and parking enforcement strategies we employ are designed to be effective, fair and customer-friendly.

Air

Parking Management at Airports - revenue protection and security are high priorities for airport car parks. Both are areas in which we excel. CP Plus can offer increased levels of security, including regular monitoring by uniformed staff, CCTV, and the latest automatic number plate recognition (ANPR) technology, as well as a range of suitable charging and enforcement procedures.
