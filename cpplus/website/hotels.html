Car park management - hotels & leisure

Whether you are involved in the management of a hotel or restaurant chain, multiplex cinema or leisure centre, stadium or any other big entertainment or leisure venue, wherever you are based in the UK, we can help you with car park security and parking management.

If parking abuse is a problem, we can bring parking control by using our proven STEP (STaged Enforcement Policy) approach, combined with modern monitoring, number plate recognition and tracking technologies.

We can also help you make the most of your parking assets. Empty parking spaces may represent wasted revenue opportunities. We can advise on ways of maximising parking usage, at times of the day when your car park may be underused.

For anyone in the hotel and leisure industry, managing and operating car park facilities is a non-core activity. For CP Plus, Car Park Management and Car Park Security are the areas in which we both specialise and excel.
