Car Parking Technology

CP Plus utilises the latest technology to ensure that our clients and their customers benefit from a superior parking experience. Our in-house developed and designed permit management, patrol logging and vehicle tracking systems provide our clients with the most advanced technology available.

Staff use the latest handheld computer technology to support parking enforcement processes and as part of our permit system. State-of-the-art technologies, such as mobile phone and Internet-based payment systems and automatic number plate recognition (ANPR), are also incorporated in the portfolio of services we offer.

We use technology where there is a proven need and where it adds real value to the services on offer - not because it's the latest thing. We never underestimate the simple approach. Where the situation demands it, we employ uncomplicated, traditional systems - in fact we utilise whatever is needed to ensure that our client receives the very highest Car Park Management service.
