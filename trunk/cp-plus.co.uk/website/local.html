Car park management - local authorities

Local Authorities are responsible for providing a variety of services to the local community. At CP Plus we are responsible for providing one kind of service - and we do that exceptionally well. We are experts in operating and managing car parks and it is our goal to accommodate the requirements of all our clients.

With our help, local authorities can improve the parking services they provide, regenerate car parks in need of a face-lift and please their council taxpayers - all without any capital investment. What's more, they can do all these while increasing revenues and sometimes generating brand new revenue streams.

How is this achieved? Through the benefits of PFI/PPP projects, both large and small. Private involvement in public sector projects can bring in much-needed funding, but it also adds much more. With PFI/PPP, local authorities can take advantage of the full range of private sector design, construction, management and commercial skills.

Funding, managing and operating parking services and facilities is our business, co-ordinating services for the community is yours. Together we can achieve both, to a higher standard than ever before.
