Car park management - healthcare

Providing parking management in a healthcare environment means combining sympathy with strength, compassion with control.

Our healthcare parking management schemes are designed to take account of high levels of vehicle turnover, security issues and staff parking needs, while also being sensitive to the stresses and strains that patients and their families may be experiencing.

A shared ethos is the cornerstone of CP Plus's success in the healthcare sector. As the UK's No.1 Car Park Management & Security Service Provider to the NHS, we understand that hospital trusts and others in the sector have very special requirements. We work closely with healthcare professionals to ensure that we provide a first class service, tailored to precise requirements.

Our experience of managing and controlling over 40 NHS sites for 17 Trusts ensures that we understand the importance of keeping vital road arteries clear of vehicles and have the practical skills to deal with major incidents and other emergency procedures.

CP Plus is able to fund both large and smaller-scale PFI/PPP projects, enabling trusts to provide improved levels of service and facilities without draining valuable resources needed for healthcare, while at the same time creating opportunities for them to generate revenue.

As well as patient and staff parking, such schemes may cover security management, including, for example, the funding, installation and implementation of tailor-made CCTV and control centre systems.
