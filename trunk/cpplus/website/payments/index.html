Parking Charge Notice (PCN) payments

If you have received a PCN please click on the button below to make a payment by Debit or Credit card.

Please ensure that you have the PCN number (7 digits in the top right hand corner of the PCN) available to enter. Please note that you will be charged a convenience fee of £1.50 for this service.

If your PCN is an 11 digit number starting 04, please call the following number to make payment - 08445 454 645.
