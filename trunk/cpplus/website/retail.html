Car park management - retail

At CP Plus we understand the parking management needs of the retail industry and share your business focus. Like you, we are in a service-based industry.

Our considerable track record in managing parking at a wide variety of retail schemes gives us the expertise and experience to advise on the right solutions for your business.

A reliable and safe parking environment may be necessary for your operation but it is not your core business - but it is ours.

We appreciate that each supermarket, shopping centre and retail park has a different set of priorities and we adapt our parking services to reflect those differences, either on a store-by-store or national basis. We can also advise on extra revenue generation, where we feel parking assets are being under-utilised.

Car park management

Car Park Management - Supermarkets - We work with you, systematically and sensitively, to provide a more professional and efficiently managed parking environment. While we take steps to minimise parking abuse, our emphasis is on avoiding heavy-handed enforcement measures in favour of promoting a more positive approach to good parking behaviour.

Car Park Management - Shopping centres and retail parks - In conjunction with our clients, we devise schemes that take account of individual site needs. Where a site includes restaurants, cinemas and other attractions, for example, we allow for variable and extended times and can operate redemption schemes to enable clients to refund or discount the cost of parking to their customers. On communal sites, we can act as a single, focal point of control, simplifying and integrating all your parking management and security processes.
